Chicken Soup

This is a recipe to create a bowl of tasty chicken soup. I mean it's a chicken soup, what could go wrong with a simple chicken-made-into-soup. Just check the original recipe through the link below, and you'll get what I mean.

Original Recipe of Chicken Soup.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Drumsticks
  • 4L Water
  • 1 tbs Salt
  • 1 tbs Sugar
  • 1 tsp White Pepper

Directions

  1. Blanch chicken drumsticks.
  2. Put chicken drumsticks in 4L of water, bring to boil with low heat.
  3. Once boiling, add in salt, sugar and white pepper.
  4. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes.

Chicken Katsu

This is a recipe to create Chicken Katsu, a breaded chicken fillet. Again, it's not hard, just click on the link below to find the original recipe.

Original Recipe of Chicken Katsu.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Breast Fillet
  • 1 cup Panko
  • 1 tbs Flour
  • 1 pc Egg

Directions

  1. Cut chicken breast thinly, dust with flour.
  2. Dip into beaten egg batter.
  3. Then into panko.
  4. Bake in oven, or deep fry.

Gado-Gado

This is a recipe to create a complicated salad. Indonesian salad with peanut sauce dressing.

Original Recipe of Gado-Gado.

Ingredients

  • 200g Green Beans
  • 60g Bean Sprouts
  • 200g Potatoes
  • 2 pcs Fried Tofu
  • 1 cup Peanut Sauce

Directions

  1. Blanch green beans, potatoes and tofu.
  2. Chop them into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Add bean sprouts.
  4. Add and mix with peanut sauce.

Nasi Goreng

This is a recipe to create Nasi Goreng, which literally translates to fried rice.

Original Recipe of Nasi Goreng.

Ingredients

  • 150g Sliced Chicken Fillet
  • 1 cup Cooked Rice
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 stalk Spring Onion
  • 1 tbs Sweet Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp White Pepper
  • 1 pc Egg

Directions

  1. Saute garlic.
  2. Crack egg into hot oil in a wok.
  3. Stir fry chicken until meat turns white.
  4. Add cooked rice, stir well and add sweet soy sauce.
  5. Add salt and white pepper, mix well.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onion.

Chicken Satay

This is a recipe to create chicken satay, Indonesian style. Again, with peanut sauce.

Original Recipe of Chicken Satay.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Thigh Fillet
  • 10 Wooden Skewers
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tbs Sweet Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp White Pepper
  • 1 cup Peanut Sauce

Directions

  1. Chopped chicken, marinate with sweet soy sauce and chopped garlic.
  2. Thread chicken pieces into skewers.
  3. Grill over charcoal or BBQ.
  4. Serve with peanut sauce.
